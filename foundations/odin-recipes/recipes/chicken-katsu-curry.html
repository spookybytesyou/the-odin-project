Chicken Katsu Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Oil

Description

Chicken katsu curry is a popular Japanese dish featuring crispy, panko-breaded chicken cutlets served with a rich, savory curry sauce and steamed rice. The chicken is typically deep-fried until golden brown and crispy, providing a satisfying contrast to the smooth, flavorful curry. It's a comforting and delicious meal that combines the textures of crispy fried chicken with the aromatic and mildly spiced Japanese curry.

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
