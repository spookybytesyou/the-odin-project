Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring wide, flat pasta layered with savory fillings and baked. Typically, it includes layers of pasta interspersed with meat sauce (like ragù), béchamel sauce, cheese (such as ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and seasonings. The dish is often topped with grated cheese that melts during baking, resulting in a rich, casserole-like meal.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
